Custom Strategy Development

For traders who already have a concept or set of rules. We translate your discretionary logic into a robust, automated TradingView strategy.

  • Translating rules into precise Pine Script logic.
  • Advanced entry & exit conditions.
  • Risk management modules (position sizing, SL/TP).
  • Backtesting & performance evaluation.
  • Support across multiple markets and timeframes.

Perfect if: You have a manual strategy you want to automate or backtest.

// Strategy Logic
strategy.entry("Long", strategy.long, when = longCondition)
strategy.exit("Exit", "Long", stop = sl_price, limit = tp_price)

Indicator & Signal Design

We build custom indicators that provide clear buy/sell signals, visual overlays, and alert-ready conditions for automation.

  • Buy/Sell signal generation.
  • Visual overlays (Supply/Demand, Order Blocks, VWAP).
  • Alert-ready conditions for webhooks & bots.
  • Multi-timeframe dashboards.

Perfect if: You need visual confirmation or alerts for your trading.

// Indicator Logic
