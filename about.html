About Kodologic

Bridging the gap between discretionary trading and automated precision.

Our Story

Kodologic began as a passion project to solve a simple problem: most trading tools are either too simple to be useful or too complex to be usable.

We are a boutique studio focused on high-quality, bespoke algorithmic development. We don't just write code; we understand market structure, risk management, and the psychological challenges of trading.

Over the years, we have worked with traders from around the world—from individual retail traders to small funds—turning their unique edges into robust, automated systems.

Our Values

  • Clarity: No black boxes. You should understand exactly how your tools work.
  • Honesty: We don't promise "100% win rates." We focus on probability and risk.
  • Quality: Clean, efficient, and well-documented code is our standard.
  • Education: We help you understand the logic behind the code.
